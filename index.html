About Khoon-e-Hayaat

Connecting blood donors with recipients and blood banks to save lives across Pakistan since 2020.

Our Mission

Khoon-e-Hayaat is committed to ensuring no one dies due to lack of blood. We bridge the gap between voluntary blood donors and those in need through innovative technology and community engagement.

Every day, countless lives are saved through blood transfusions, yet the demand consistently exceeds supply. Our platform makes it easy for donors to register and be matched with those needing blood.

Blood Donation Facts

0

Lives saved annually in Pakistan through blood transfusions highlighting the critical role of voluntary donors in supporting patients with accidents, surgeries, cancer, and blood disorders

0

Around 65% of blood donations in Pakistan come from replacement donors, usually family or friends of patients, highlighting the urgent need to increase voluntary, regular blood donations for a safer supply.

0

The minimum waiting period between whole blood donations is 56 days, allowing the body enough time to replenish lost red blood cells and maintain healthy iron levels.

0

A single unit of donated blood can help up to 3 different patients when separated into components such as red cells, plasma, and platelets, each serving a unique life-saving purpose
